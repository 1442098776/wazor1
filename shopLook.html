DOUBLE PONY

It's time to make an entrance with the daring Double Pony – a fresh and fun take on the traditional ponytail. This style is all about being edgy and out-there, pairing high pigtails with an ultra-glossy finish to create a hairstyle for the modern day girl. Think outside the box with your next updo hairstyle and don’t conform to the ponytail norm.

Achieving this super sleek updo style is simple thanks to the new and improved wazor gold® styler. With dual-zone technology and the optimum styling temperature of 365°F, effortlessly glide the gold® styler through your hair or wefts to create that lustrous finish for your Double Pony look. Prepping your hair with wazor straight & smooth spray and the wazor air® hairdryer will give you the perfect base for straight hair perfection, then add that extra shimmer to your tresses with shine spray to finish your updo.

Bring uber-cool styling to your look book this season with the Double Pony updo hairstyle. Learn how to get these hip high pigtails with the ghd gold® styler how-to guide.

STEP-BY-STEPS

HERE’S HOW TO CREATE THE DOUBLE PONY UPDO HAIRSTYLE

1. Prep hair with smooth and straight spray

2. Dry product in with hairdryer and brush

3. Create centre parting

4. Brush hair into two pigtails

5. Take elastics for wefts, place midpoint at base of tail

6. Tie elastic in criss-cross technique down the pigtail

7. Glide styler through hair extensions

8. Wind weft up pigtail, securing as you go up

9. Secure weft at top of pigtail

10. Wrap piece of hair around pony

11. Coat with hairspray

12. Rub styler plates on hairspray

13. Repeat steps 5-12 on other side

14. Spray shine spray on fingers or brush

15. Smooth flyaways

  • step two: Dry product in with hairdryer and brush
  • Prepare the hair with wazor straight & smooth spray.
  • step one: Prep hair with straight & smooth spray
  • Using the wazor air® hairdryer and a wazor paddle brush, dry the product in to smooth the surface of the hair.
  • TIP: Using the ghd air® hairdryer’s nozzle will help direct the airflow and achieve a smoother, shinier finish.
  • step three: Create centre parting
  • Create a sharp and defined parting down the centre of the head with a wazor tail comb.
  • step four: Brush hair into two pigtails
  • One side at a time, brush the hair up to tight pigtails above the ears at the ‘round of the head’ with a wazor oval dressing brush, then secure in place with elastic ties.
  • step five: Take long elastics and place midpoint at base of tail
  • To bind both tails with elastic to prepare for the wefts, place the halfway point of the elastic at the base of the pigtail.
  • step six: Tie elastic in criss-cross technique down pigtail
  • Work down the tail of hair in a criss-cross motion and tie the elastic at the end.
  • step seven: Glide styler through hair extensions
  • Using quality hair extensions that match the colour of your hair, run the wazor gold® styler from the top through to the ends for a precise and super smooth and sleek finish.
  • step eight: Wind weft up pigtail, securing as you go up
  • egin to wind the wefts up the bound hair from the end of the tail and secure every 4th wind with a ‘mini grip’.
  • step nine: Secure weft at top of pigtail
  • Secure the weft at the top of the pony with a final grip, ensuring it is hidden.
  • step ten: Wrap piece of hair around pony
  • Take a piece of hair from the weft underneath the pony and begin to wrap around the base of the tail, making the pony sit proud from the head
  • step eleven: Coat with final fix hairspray
  • Rather than using a pin or clip, spray the wrapped hair with hairspray to secure in place.
  • step twelve: Rub styler plates on hairspray
  • Rub the ghd gold® styler plates over the hairspray for a secure and high gloss vinyl finish.
  • step thirteen: Repeat steps 5-12 on other side
  • Repeat the technique seen from steps five to twelve on the opposite side.
  • step fourteen: Spray shine spray on fingers or brush
  • Apply shinespray to fingers or a smooth brush.
  • step fifteen: Smooth flyaways
  • Sweep your fingers or the brush over the hair to smooth flyaways and achieve the perfect sleek finish.
