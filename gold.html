WAZOR GOLD® FEATURES

Take a closer look at thewazor gold® styler,with innovative dual-zone technology for a premium performance, an improved heat-up time of only 25 seconds, a modernized design for smooth, snag-free styling and automatic sleep mode for peace of mind.

1. Smooth, contoured floating plates:Glide through hair for quick, frizz-free styling and enhanced salon shine.

2. Sleek design and round barrel:For versatile, snag-free styling, quickly and easily.

 

3. Heated to 365 degrees: The optimum temperature for styling; any hotter damages the hair, any cooler and the results are compromised.

4. Dual-zone technology:Two heat sensors control heat to the optimum temperature for smoother, sleeker and healthier looking hair*.

5. Sleep mode: Switches off if not used for 30 minutes - for safety and ease of mind!
